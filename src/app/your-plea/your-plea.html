Charge Public service vehicle – passenger failing to produce a ticket

View charge information

On 08/10/2013 at Barnet Hill EN5 being a passenger on a Public Service Vehicle operated on behalf of London Bus Services Limited being used for the carriage of passengers at separate fares did fail to produce a ticket when requested to do so by the Driver Inspector or Conductor.

Contrary to regulation 7(2)(E) of the Public Service Vehicles (Conduct of Drivers, Inspectors, Conductors and Passengers) Regulations 1990 and section 25 of the Public Passenger Vehicles Act 1981.
